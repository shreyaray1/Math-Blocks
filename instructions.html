MathBlocks

Welcome to MathBlocks! This game's purpose is to train your speed in solving algebra equations.

There are 3 levels - easy, medium, and hard. The easy level contains simple linear equations where you solve for x, such as 2x - 3 = 9. The medium level contains quadratic equations which are slightly more difficult (they will only have one answer), such as x^2 + 2x + 1. The hard level is the most difficult and is consisted of other polynomials, such at x^3 - 8 = 0.

Once you press the start button in the game, blocks with equations written on them will begin to drop. You have to answer them before they reach the bottom of the white box. There will be three radio buttons above each question, and if you pick the one with the right answer, a new equation will appear at the top once more. If you pick the one with the wrong answer the equation box will disappear and you will lose a life. The goal is to get the most correct answers before you lose three lives. You lose a life if you cannot answer the question before it reaches the ground.

Have fun!
